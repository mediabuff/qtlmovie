Download FFmpeg binaries for Mac OS X:
  http://evermeet.cx/ffmpeg/

Sample download links:
  http://evermeet.cx/ffmpeg/ffmpeg-3.0.2.7z
  http://evermeet.cx/ffmpeg/ffprobe-3.0.2.7z

Decompression commands:
  brew install p7zip
  7z e ffmpeg-3.0.2.7z 
  7z e ffprobe-3.0.2.7z

Extract the following files here:
  ffmpeg
  ffprobe

Pay attention to other FFmpeg ports to Mac. Some of them do not include
the "subtitles" filter, which make them unusable with QtlMovie.
